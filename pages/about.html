BUILT ON TRUST


We are the most honest, having worked for over 20 years with an excellent reputation.



The company was founded in 1960 by Robert Steffes who primarily sold farm equipment and livestock. In 1984, the business incorporated and moved all business operations to Fargo, North Dakota. From that time forward, the company has experienced significant growth and an expansion of its service offerings. We now have locations in North Dakota, South Dakota, Minnesota, Iowa, Colorado, and Kansas and have representatives serving all of the Midwest and Rocky Mountain Regions of the United States.
